Vibrant Festivals Across India

Lohri

13 January

Lohri is a midwinter folk and harvest festival that marks the passing of the winter solstice and the end of winter.

Pongal

January

A harvest festival in Tamil Nadu, celebrating abundance with traditional feasts.

Holi

March

The festival of colors where joy and laughter paint every street with rainbow hues.

Navratri

Sept-Oct

Navratri is a nine-night festival honoring Goddess Durga.

Diwali

Oct–Nov

The Festival of Lights honors good over evil with lamps, sweets, and fireworks.

Christmas

25 Dec

It is celebrated every year on December 25 to mark the birth of Jesus Christ, the Son of God, as per Christians.
